# Vision

## Who is this for?

Players who want a 20-minute-run roguelite that feels responsive and stat-buildy — the "one more run" audience of Brotato, 20MTD, The Binding of Isaac. Not for players who want long-session ARPGs with crafting.

## What is it?

A top-down hack-and-slash roguelite where you build a stat-heavy character across short runs, with meaningful weapon and stat choices every level.

## Pillars

1. **Short, stat-dense runs.** Every pickup changes the math noticeably. No filler loot.
2. **Readable combat.** Damage, crit, status effects — all visible on screen through popups and clear audio.
3. **Weapon identity matters.** A sword and a spear feel different, not just different DPS.
4. **No reflex gates.** Execution is simple; decisions are hard. The challenge is build choice, not twitch precision.
5. **Runs end cleanly.** Death returns you to menu in under 5 seconds; restart in under 10.

## What this game is NOT

- Not a deckbuilder. No cards, no piles, no draw mechanics.
- Not a precision platformer. Jumping and dodging should never be the hard part.
- Not an ARPG with crafting, auction houses, or item customization.
